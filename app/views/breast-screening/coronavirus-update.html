Coronavirus (COVID-19) updates to breast screening - Breast screening (mammogram)

Breast screening services are running.

You may br prioritised if:

  • you've been told you're at very high risk of getting breast cancer
  • you're 53 or over and have never had a screening invitation
  • you're over 70 but have not had a final invitation for screening

It's important to go to your appointment unless you or someone you live with has symptoms of COVID-19.
