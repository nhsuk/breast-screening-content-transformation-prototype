Breast screening (mammogram)

Further help and support - Breast screening (mammogram)

You may need more help and support with breast screening for many reasons.

Speak to the GP surgery or call the breast screening service if you have any questions about breast screening invitations or results.

Information and support from the NHS Breast Screening Programme

You can contact the Public Health England Screening Helpdesk if you have any questions about breast screening practice or policy (England only) by:

  • calling the helpdesk on 020 3682 0890
  • emailing PHE.screeninghelpdesk@nhs.net

The PHE helpdesk does not have access to medical records and is not able to provide medical advice or screening results.

Speak to the GP surgery or call the breast screening service where you had the mammogram for information about your breast screening mammogram or results.
